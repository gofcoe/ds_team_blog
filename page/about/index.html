About

Hi! This is the blog for the Data Science Team at the Smart Data Foundry. We use this blog to share technical outputs and lessons we’ve learned, we hope you find it useful.

The team is comprised of:

  • Nick Radcliffe
  • David Tracy
  • Pei Shan Yu
  • Paola Arce
  • Mike Spencer
  • Victor Diaz
  • Jack MacKenzie
  • Eric Janto
  • Fiona Rasanga

We come from a wide range of backgrounds, but are united by our enthusiasm for data and the tools that help improve its use. Some of our favourite tools include Python, R, Git and Bash. These fundamental building blocks are especially good because the allow us to build what we need to solve a given problem.

To find out more about the Smart Data Foundry, visit our website. The short version is: We’re a mission-led organisation here to unlock the positive power of financial data to improve people’s lives.
